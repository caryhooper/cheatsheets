ASM Commands/Patterns----
ESP----Points to the top of the stack
EBP----Used to calculate an address relative to another address.  Aka frame pointer.

push----Pushes data on top of the stack.  Only changes the value of ESP.  
pop----removes data from the top of the stack.  Only changes the value of ESP.

The Stack----The stack grows up | LIFO tech
The Stack----grows toward lower-numbered addresses



mov eax,[ebp+10h]----Move a dword from 16bytes (hex 10) down the stack into EAX.
