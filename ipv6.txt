####IPv6 Commands and Attacks

Ping multicast IPv6 for Discovery----ping6 -c 2 ff02::1%eth0
Show IPv6 neighbors----ip -6 neigh
Nmap IPv6----nmap -Fnq6 fe80::139%eth0
Netcat IPv6----ncat fe80::139%eth0 110
